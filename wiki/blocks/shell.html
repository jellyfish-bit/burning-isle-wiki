# Magmatic Shells

In the Basalt Deltas, an intriguing variety of shells awaits discovery, distinguished by four distinct sizes: Big, Medium, Small, and Very Small. These Magmatic Shells, scattered throughout the landscape, hold within them a trove of treasures, often unveiling glistening Pearls.
